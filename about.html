Reasons For Choosing The Topic

There are many reasons why people chose to pet sit, and quite often, it’s that people can’t have pets “in real life”. And so do I. It could be that their job or location makes it tricky to keep a dog, or they house-share with a friend who has cat allergies. Pet sitting is a brilliant way to experience the joy of having a pet, without actually having a pet.
